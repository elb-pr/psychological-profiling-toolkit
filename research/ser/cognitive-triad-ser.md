## Evidence Synthesis

### Finding 1: Core Framework Validity
Beck (1967) proposed three dimensions of depressogenic cognition: negative view of self,
world, and future. Empirically validated across clinical and non-clinical samples.
Cognitive Triad Inventory (CTI) shows good internal consistency (α=.90–.95) and
discriminates depression from anxiety [web:301][web:309].
Han Chinese women longitudinal study (2013) confirms triad structure and clinical
predictors replicate cross-culturally [web:300].
CLASSIFICATION: ESTABLISHED

### Finding 2: Triad as Mediator — Longitudinal Validity
Three-wave longitudinal study confirms cognitive triad mediates between negative events
and depressive symptoms in Hopelessness model. Triad dimensions are temporally stable
and predict future depression above and beyond baseline symptoms [web:304][web:307][web:310].
CLASSIFICATION: ESTABLISHED

### Finding 3: Documentary Proxy — CAVE Technique (Critical)
Peterson, Luborsky & Seligman (1983) and Peterson et al. (1992) developed the
Content Analysis of Verbatim Explanations (CAVE) — extracts explanatory style dimensions
(permanence, pervasiveness, personalisation) from any naturally occurring verbatim text.
Correlation with direct ASQ administration: r=0.71 [web:308][web:311].
Applied to historical speeches, interviews, letters, diaries. No subject access required.
Inter-rater reliability for CAVE coding: r=.85–.93 across trained coders.
CLASSIFICATION: ESTABLISHED for explanatory style; EMERGING for full triad all three poles

Semantic distance NLP study (2016): ME–SADNESS, ME–PAST, ME–FUTURE semantic distances
are strongest textual predictors of depressogenic cognition in natural language [web:296].
NLP dataset built on Beck's cognitive triad structure from social media text (2021) [web:299].
Online peer support forum study: triad dimensions detectable in naturalistic forum posts
with structured coding [web:302].

### Finding 4: Contradictions
- Triad specificity to depression vs anxiety debated: some studies show triad equally
  elevated in anxiety. Nature: EMPIRICAL. Both positions evidence-based.
- Explanatory style (Seligman) and cognitive triad (Beck) measure overlapping but
  distinct constructs. Documentary inference may conflate them. Nature: DEFINITIONAL.

### Evidence Gaps
1. No validation of full three-pole triad (self/world/future) from documentary sources
   simultaneously — CAVE captures explanatory style (one dimension of each pole), not
   the full triad structure.
2. No inter-rater reliability study for documentary triad coding outside CAVE paradigm.

## Transferability Verdict
VIABLE — CAVE technique is a fully validated documentary inference method for
explanatory style (permanence, pervasiveness, personalisation). Full three-pole
cognitive triad from documentary sources requires compound proxy approach.
Transfer gap: MODERATE for full triad; MINOR for explanatory style component.
